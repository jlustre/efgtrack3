# Certified Field Mentor (CFM) Trainee Checklist

Structured mentoring checklist for Certified Field Mentors guiding trainees through assignment, orientation, licensing, compliance, training, field apprenticeship, and rank advancement.

---

## Phase 1 – Assignment & Initial Contact

**Target:** Within 24–48 hours of assignment

### Mentor Acceptance

- [ ] Confirm CFM assignment
- [ ] Review trainee profile
- [ ] Review sponsor information
- [ ] Review trainee location and timezone
- [ ] Review registration status

### First Contact

- [ ] Send welcome email
- [ ] Send welcome text message
- [ ] Place welcome phone call
- [ ] Introduce yourself as CFM
- [ ] Explain your role and expectations
- [ ] Schedule initial orientation meeting

---

## Phase 2 – Orientation & Goal Setting

**Target:** Week 1

### Orientation Meeting

- [ ] Conduct orientation session
- [ ] Explain EFGTrack portal
- [ ] Review onboarding process
- [ ] Review FAP requirements
- [ ] Review licensing process
- [ ] Review compliance requirements
- [ ] Review compensation overview
- [ ] Explain rank advancement pathway

### Goal Setting

- [ ] Discuss personal goals
- [ ] Discuss financial goals
- [ ] Discuss business goals
- [ ] Set first 30-day goals
- [ ] Set first 90-day goals
- [ ] Document goals in EFGTrack

---

## Phase 3 – Licensing Progress

**Target:** Week 1–4

### Licensing Setup

- [ ] Verify licensing jurisdiction
- [ ] Verify resident state/province
- [ ] Verify licensing requirements
- [ ] Create licensing plan

### Licensing Activities

- [ ] Enroll in licensing course
- [ ] Complete licensing training
- [ ] Complete practice exams
- [ ] Schedule licensing exam
- [ ] Pass licensing exam
- [ ] Submit license application
- [ ] Verify license issuance

### Licensing Tracking

- [ ] Upload license documents
- [ ] Upload certification documents
- [ ] Update licensing status
