# 1. Getting started

## What is EFGTrack?

EFGTrack is your agency operating system. It connects the work you do every day — prospecting, FNAs, licensing, training, mentoring, and production — into one place so you, your CFM, and your leadership can see progress and take action.

## Signing in

1. Open your agency’s EFGTrack URL (for example `https://your-agency.efgtrack.com`).
2. Sign in with the email and password provided at registration.
3. Complete email verification if prompted.
4. On first login, you may see a **profile completion** prompt. Fill in required fields so your team can support you.

## The main layout

After login you land on **My Dashboard**. The screen has three main areas:

| Area | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Top bar** | Global search, notifications bell, and your account menu |
| **Left sidebar** | Main navigation — grouped by Trackers, My Team, Communications, Resources, and Admin |
| **Main content** | The page you are working on |

### Sidebar groups

- **Top links** — Dashboard, Profile, Messages, CFM tools, Help (based on your role)
- **Trackers** — Personal progress checklists and performance tools
- **My Team** — Downline, prospects, recruiting, FNA (leaders and producers)
- **Communications** — Announcements, events, calendar, notifications
- **Resources** — Documents, videos, links, forms
- **Admin Management** — Setup and user management (admins and leaders only)

Click a group name to expand or collapse it. Your last-open groups are remembered in the browser.

## Global search

Use the **search box** in the top bar to find:

- Team members (if you have team access)
- Prospects
- Resource documents and videos
- Training courses
- Tasks
- Calendar events
- Announcements

Type at least two characters. Results are grouped by type. You only see items your role allows.

## Notifications

Click the **bell icon** for alerts about tasks, mentoring, training, announcements, rank progress, and more. Open **Notifications** in the sidebar for the full list and preferences (email, SMS, push where enabled).

# 3. Application map

High-level modules and how they connect:

```
Registration & Onboarding
        ↓
Licensing + Training + FAP (Field Apprenticeship)
        ↓
Production (Prospects → FNA → Clients) + Recruiting
        ↓
Goals & Performance + Rank Advancement
        ↓
Ongoing Compliance & Renewals
```

**Sales path:** Prospect Management → FNA → production posted on profile / Production Dashboard  
**Recruiting path:** Recruiting Pipeline → registration invitation → onboarding  
**Mentoring path:** CFM Portal ↔ trainee checklists, FNA review, goals coaching  
**Learning path:** Training Center + program trackers (onboarding, licensing, FAP, CFM training)

# 5. Trackers

## My Dashboard (`/dashboard`)

Your home base after login.

- **Stat cards** — Profile completion, onboarding, licensing, FAP, training, prospects, production, and more (varies by role).
- **Development Journey** — Progress widgets with next steps for each program.
- **Team overview** — Leaders see downline health, hot prospects, compliance alerts.
- **Profile completion** — Prompt to fill missing required fields.

Click any stat card (where linked) to see member lists or open the related tracker.

## My Profile (`/profile`)

Keep your record accurate for the agency and your upline.

| Tab / section | What to maintain |
|---|---|
| **Basic info** | Name, email, phone, location, timezone, bio |
| **Licenses** | Jurisdictions where you hold insurance licenses |
| **Production** | Post annual premium entries (feeds Production Dashboard and goals) |
| **Recruits & invitations** | Registration invite links and pending invitations |
| **Checklists** | Quick view of program checklist status |
| **Profile photo** | Headshot for directory and CFM tools |

## My Tasks (`/tasks`)

Your task command center.

| View | Use for |
|---|---|
| **List** | Sort and filter all tasks |
| **Board** | Kanban by status |
| **Calendar** | Due dates on a calendar |
| **Team** | Tasks you assigned or that involve your downline |

**Stat cards** show total, due today, overdue, completed this week, high priority, and assigned to you.

**Tips:**
- Use filters for priority, status, assignee, and date range.
- Add comments on tasks for async collaboration.
- Tasks can be created manually or generated from prospect stage automations.

## My Onboarding (`/onboarding`)

Step-by-step checklist for new associates: orientation, agreements, system setup, and first actions. Mark steps complete as you finish them. Some steps require CFM or leader **review** before they count as done.

## Licensing Tracker (`/licensing`)

Tracks your path to becoming licensed: study, exam, application, appointments, and related milestones. Progress rolls up to your Dashboard and CFM visibility.

## Compliance & Renewals (`/compliance`)

**After** initial licensing — ongoing compliance:

| Record type | Examples |
|---|---|
| State / provincial license | Renewals by jurisdiction |
| E&O insurance | Policy expiration |
| AML training | Refresher due dates |
| Carrier appointments | Contracting renewals |
| Continuing education | CE credits by period |

- **Sync from profile licenses** — Pulls jurisdictions from your profile.
- **Add record** — Manual E&O, AML, CE, etc.
- Leaders with licensing permissions can **verify** records.

## Field Apprenticeship (`/field-apprenticeship`)

The FAP checklist for trainees. Work through phases with your CFM. Completion feeds rank advancement and CFM effectiveness metrics.

## CFM Training (`/cfm-training`)

Checklist for associates pursuing **Certified Field Mentor** certification. Separate from the CFM Portal day-to-day tools.

## Production Dashboard (`/team/production`)

One dashboard for everyone; content adapts to your role.

# 10. Common workflows

## Register a new recruit

1. **Recruiting Pipeline** → Add recruit candidate (or Prospect Management with recruiting funnel).
2. Move through kanban stages as you present the opportunity.
3. Send **registration invitation** from profile or prospect record.
4. Recruit completes registration → appears in **Active recruit journey**.
5. Monitor **Onboarding** and **Licensing** trackers.

## Run a client appointment (sales)

1. Add prospect in **Prospect Management**.
2. Log calls and meetings on the prospect record.
3. Create **FNA** from prospect or FNA Management.
4. Complete FNA wizard (or send **client portal invite**).
5. Move prospect on **Pipeline board** toward application / client.
6. Post production on **My Profile** when policy issues.
7. Activity may auto-update **Goals** scorecard.

## CFM weekly trainee review

1. **CFM Portal** — Check trainee checklist progress and risk flags.
2. **FNA Review Queue** — Review submitted FNAs.
3. **Goals → CFM Coaching** — Coach off-track goals.
4. **Training → Coaching** — Verify course completion.
5. Log mentoring notes and schedule next session via **Bookings**.

## Renew a license or E&O

1. Open **Compliance & Renewals**.
2. Sync from profile or edit the existing record.
3. Update expiration date and upload notes when renewed.
4. Leader verifies if your agency requires verification.

## Plan your month (production focus)

1. **Goals → Performance Planner** — Set income/production target.
2. Review funnel math (contacts → appointments → sales).
3. Create activity goals or use **Activity Scorecard**.
4. Track prospects in **Prospect Management** follow-up center.
5. Review **Production Dashboard** weekly.

# 11. Permissions and visibility

You only see menu items and data your **role** and **permissions** allow.

| Concept | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Member, CFM, team leader, agency owner, admin, etc. |
| **Permission** | Fine-grained ability (e.g. `manage prospects`, `view own team`) |
| **Downline scope** | Leaders see their hierarchy, not the whole company (unless admin) |
| **Prospect privacy** | Prospects are owned by the associate who created them unless shared |
| **CFM access** | CFM Portal and trainee tools require CFM role or assignment |

If you need access to something you cannot see, contact your team leader or agency administrator.

# 12. Tips for daily use

1. **Start on the Dashboard** — Check journey widgets and stat cards each morning.
2. **Clear follow-ups** — Prospect Management follow-up center and Tasks due today.
3. **Keep profile current** — Licenses and production entries feed multiple modules.
4. **Use global search** — Faster than hunting through menus.
5. **Set notification preferences** — Avoid alert fatigue; keep critical channels on.
6. **Favorite resources** — Build a personal quick library in Documents and Videos.
7. **One CRM per purpose** — Insurance prospects vs recruiting pipeline are separate by design.
8. **Check Help & Support** — Module guides and tickets when stuck.
